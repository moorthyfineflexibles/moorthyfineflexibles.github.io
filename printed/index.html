Laminated Film Roll Raw Material


Our laminated film roll raw materials are tailored for manufacturers in the food industry who require reliable, food-safe packaging solutions. These rolls are composed of high-grade polyester combined with materials like BOPP, PE, or metalized films to ensure excellent sealing strength, freshness retention, and resistance to contamination. Suitable for pouch making, sachets, and other flexible packaging forms, our rolls are available in various thicknesses and sizes to suit your production needs. With high-performance barrier properties and smooth printability, our films offer both protection and an attractive presentation for a wide range of food products.

Polyester Laminated Printing Covers


We specialize in high-quality polyester laminated printing covers designed specifically for food packaging applications. These covers not only enhance the visual appeal of your products but also offer essential protection and durability. With excellent clarity and vibrant color reproduction, our laminated covers help your brand stand out on shelves while maintaining freshness and hygiene. Ideal for snacks, spices, dry fruits, and other food items, our covers are made to withstand handling, moisture, and temperature variations. Whether you need custom designs or standard packaging, we ensure a premium look with strong barrier properties.
