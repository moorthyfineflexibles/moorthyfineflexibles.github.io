About Us


Our company was started in 1993, our factory has grown into a trusted and experienced name in the flexible packaging industry, specializing in rotogravure printed bags. With over three decades of expertise, we have continually evolved to meet the changing demands of our clients while maintaining a strong commitment to quality, precision, and innovation.

At the heart of our operations is the rotogravure printing process—a high-speed, high-quality printing method that allows for vibrant, detailed designs with excellent color consistency. This makes our printed bags ideal for branding and retail packaging across various industries, including food, consumer goods, and more.

To enhance both appearance and functionality, our facility is also equipped with advanced lamination technology. This process adds a protective layer to the printed material, improving strength, durability, and resistance to moisture, making the bags suitable for demanding storage and transport conditions.

We offer customized packaging solutions tailored to each client’s specific needs. From initial design to final production, we focus on delivering products that meet both visual and functional standards. Our team is dedicated to maintaining high manufacturing standards and timely delivery, ensuring complete customer satisfaction.
