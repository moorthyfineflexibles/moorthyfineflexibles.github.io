Chairman & MD

Mr. B. Ajay Balan

Ajay Balan is the Chairman of Moorthy Fine Flexibles, a company established in 1993 and known for its steady presence in the flexible packaging industry. With a focus on practical solutions and consistent quality, he plays an active role in overseeing the company’s operations and growth.

The company specializes in rotogravure printed bags, offering customized packaging solutions for industries like food and consumer goods. Under his guidance, the factory has adopted advanced technologies such as high-speed rotogravure printing and lamination, helping to improve both the appearance and durability of packaging materials.

We work closely with the team to ensure timely delivery, reliable service, and client satisfaction. Our approach is rooted in experience, attention to detail, and a commitment to maintaining the quality and trust the company has built over the past three decades.

Moorthy Fine Flexibles continues to uphold its promise of delivering flexible packaging solutions that combine functionality, aesthetic appeal, and environmental responsibility — making it a preferred partner in the industry today and well into the future.
